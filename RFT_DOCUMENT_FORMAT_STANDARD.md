# RFT Document Format Standard

This document defines the **standardized 10-section format** for all RFT (Request for Tender) documents generated by IntelliBid's Smart RFT Builder.

### 1. Cover Page & Summary

**Purpose:** Provides essential identification and overview information

**Required Elements:**
- Project Title
- Tender Reference Number
- Issuing Organization
- Submission Deadline
- Contact Details
- Brief executive summary of the tender

---

### 2. Introduction & Background

**Purpose:** Establishes context and organizational background

**Required Elements:**
- Overview of the organization
- Purpose of the tender
- High-level project background and context
- Strategic alignment and business drivers
- Current state and desired future state

---

### 3. Scope of Work (SOW)

**Purpose:** Defines detailed requirements and deliverables

**Required Elements:**
- Detailed description of deliverables
- Functional requirements
- Technical requirements
- Expected service levels and performance criteria
- In-scope and out-of-scope items
- Dependencies and constraints

---

### 4. Objectives & Business Outcomes

**Purpose:** Articulates expected results and success criteria

**Required Elements:**
- Key goals to be achieved
- Measurable KPIs and success indicators
- Business benefits and value proposition
- Strategic outcomes
- Timeline expectations

---

### 5. Instructions to Tenderers

**Purpose:** Guides vendors on how to respond

**Required Elements:**
- Submission format (digital, email, portal)
- Tender timeline and milestones
- Clarification process and contact details
- Mandatory requirements
- Documentation requirements
- Format and structure of responses

---

### 6. Evaluation Criteria

**Purpose:** Explains how proposals will be assessed

**Required Elements:**
- Technical capability and approach
- Commercial offer and cost structure
- Compliance, risk, and quality metrics
- Scoring methodology and weightage
- Evaluation process and timeline
- Selection criteria

---

### 7. Vendor Response Templates

**Purpose:** Standardizes vendor responses for easier comparison

**Required Elements:**
- Technical response table
- Pricing and commercial proposal format
- Non-functional requirements questionnaire
- Cybersecurity and compliance checklist
- Product features questionnaire
- Agile/delivery methodology questionnaire

---

### 8. Contractual & Legal Terms

**Purpose:** Establishes legal framework and obligations

**Required Elements:**
- Draft contract or master agreement
- Confidentiality and data protection clauses
- Intellectual property rights
- Liability and indemnification terms
- Termination conditions
- Dispute resolution procedures
- Insurance and bonding requirements

---

### 9. Governance & Delivery Model

**Purpose:** Defines project management and execution framework

**Required Elements:**
- Project governance structure
- Reporting requirements and cadence
- Roles and responsibilities (RACI)
- Escalation procedures
- Issue resolution process
- Change management approach
- Quality assurance framework

---

### 10. Appendices

**Purpose:** Provides supporting reference materials

**Required Elements:**
- Acronyms and definitions
- Reference documents (policies, standards)
- Supporting diagrams or data models
- Relevant industry standards (ISO, IATA, etc.)
- Technical specifications
- Sample data or interfaces
- Regulatory requirements

---

## Guidelines for AI Generation

When generating RFT documents using this format:

1. **Consistency:** Always use these 10 sections in this exact order
2. **Completeness:** Each section should contain 3-5 comprehensive paragraphs
3. **Industry Context:** Reference relevant standards (IATA, ISO, etc.) where applicable
4. **Aviation Focus:** For Nujum Air projects, include airline-specific requirements
5. **Professional Tone:** Use formal, enterprise-grade language
6. **Clarity:** Ensure requirements are specific, measurable, and unambiguous
7. **Compliance:** Include relevant regulatory and compliance requirements
8. **Templates:** Provide clear, usable response templates for vendors

This format is used by:
- **smartRftService.ts** - Primary RFT generation service
